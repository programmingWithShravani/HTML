Tata Consultancy Services

Welcome to Tata Consultancy Services

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) is a global IT services, consulting, and business solutions
company headquartered in Mumbai, India. Founded in 1968, TCS is a subsidiary of the
Tata Group and has grown to become one of the largest IT services firms in the world.
TCS offers a wide range of services including software development, IT consulting, business process outsourcing, and digital
transformation solutions to clients across various industries globally.



Key Highlights

  • Global Presence: TCS operates in over 46 countries and has a significant presence in North America, Europe, Asia-Pacific, and Latin America.
  • Workforce: TCS employs over 500,000 professionals worldwide, making it one of the largest private-sector employers in the IT industry.
  • Revenue: TCS consistently ranks among the top IT services companies globally in terms of revenue, with annual revenues exceeding $20 billion.
  • Innovation: TCS invests heavily in research and development, focusing on emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, cloud computing, and blockchain.
  • Corporate Social Responsibility: TCS is committed to corporate social responsibility initiatives, including education, healthcare, and environmental sustainability programs.
